CAMERA MANUAL

Using ValueCam

Most camera actions are available directly on the live screen. Settings are saved automatically.

01

Shoot and review

Photo
Tap the white shutter on screen or quickly press the physical BOOT button.
Video
Tap the red REC button to start or stop recording.
Review
Hold the white shutter. Swipe left or right through photos; tap to return live.
Storage
Photos and AVI videos are saved to the microSD card.

02

Modes and settings

Choose
Tap the mode name at upper left, choose a category, then choose a mode.
Quick switch
Swipe left or right on the live image.
Adjust
Tap the lower-left settings button. Tap arrows for choices or open a value to use its slider.
Clear
Hold the live image to reset a mode that stores trails or history.

03

Exposure and power

Auto EV
Leave AUTO EV on for normal use. Turn it off only when you want a fixed exposure.
Grid
Swipe up on the live image to turn the rule-of-thirds grid on or off.
Screen
Quickly press POWER to turn the screen off or on. Hold POWER to shut down.
Restart
Hold BOOT for four seconds until RESTART READY appears, then release. RESET restarts immediately.

MODE REFERENCE

What each mode does

Descriptions refer to the live view. EV or EXPO controls exposure and is available in every mode.

Time8 modes

TREMOR

Shows changes between frames as colored motion. HOLD accumulates movement; FLSH shows the newest difference only.

GAIN · DECAY · NOISE · MAP · TIME

ECHO

Keeps earlier bright parts of the frame behind the live image to create fading motion trails.

DECAY · BLEND · CON

PAINT

Keeps the brightest value seen at each pixel, allowing lights and bright objects to build up over time.

DECAY · THRESH · CON

CHRONO

Places three delayed brightness samples into separate color channels. Movement appears as split colored edges.

DELAY · GAIN · CON

STRATA

Displays each row from a different recent moment. Moving subjects stretch or bend across the frame.

SPAN · DIR

SUNDIAL

Accumulates bright trails and assigns their color from the time they were recorded.

PERIOD

SLIT

Repeatedly copies a moving strip from the live feed into one persistent image.

WIDTH · STEP · DIR

STROBE

Compares consecutive frames and displays only areas with enough movement.

THRESH · LOOK
Art8 modes

DITHER

Converts brightness into a one-bit or two-color dither pattern using one of eight algorithms.

TYPE · SIZE · INK · DIR

VALUE

Divides the image into a brightness grid. Saved photos also include a numbered value-grid version.

COLS · ROWS · LOCK · RANGE

DOT

Builds the image from circles, squares, or diamonds whose size represents local brightness.

SIZE · LVL · SHP · INK · DIR

PALETTE

Reduces blocks of the image to the nearest color in a selected limited palette.

SIZE · PAL · MATCH

ASCII

Replaces image blocks with text characters selected by brightness.

SIZE · INK

INK

Runs edge detection and draws detected outlines as ink on a plain background.

THRESH · INK

HATCH

Uses diagonal, vertical, and horizontal lines to represent progressively darker tones.

SPACING · INK

POSTER

Reduces each color channel to a small number of levels for flat color regions.

LEVELS · SAT

SEGMENTS

DEPTH

RATIO

AMOUNT

PLANET

ZOOM · LOOK

AXIS

RECOVERY

Camera not detected?

Disconnect other serial devices, reconnect the camera directly to the computer, and try again. If automatic reset fails, hold BOOT, tap RESET, release BOOT, then reconnect.

The release includes VALUECAM_SD.BIN for microSD recovery. Rename it to VALUECAM.BIN on the card.
