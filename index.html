  1. 01

    Turn the camera on and connect its USB programming port.

  2. 02

    Use desktop Chrome or Edge, then press Connect camera.

  3. 03

    Select USB Single Serial and keep the cable connected until the camera restarts.

CAMERA MANUAL

Start shooting

ValueCam opens directly to the live view. These four actions cover normal use; the reference below handles the less frequent controls.

01

PHOTO

Tap the white shutter

Or quickly press BOOT. The photo is saved to the microSD card.

02

VIDEO

Tap the red REC button

Tap it again to stop. Video is saved as an AVI file.

03

MODE

Tap the name at upper left

Choose a category and mode, or swipe left and right to move quickly.

04

REVIEW

Hold the white shutter

Swipe through saved photos. Tap the image to return to the live view.

01

LIVE VIEW

Screen controls

MODE TITLE
Opens the category and mode picker.
SETTINGS
Opens controls for the current mode. Changes are saved automatically.
AUTO EV
Keep this on for normal use. Turn it off only when you want exposure to stay fixed.
SWIPE UP
Turns the rule-of-thirds grid on or off.
HOLD IMAGE
Clears stored trails or history in modes that accumulate frames.

02

CAMERA BODY

Physical buttons

POWER
Press: screen off or on. Hold: shut down.
BOOT
Press: take a photo. Hold 4 sec: restart when released.
RESET
Restarts the camera immediately. It does not erase photos or settings.
MICROSD
Stores photos, AVI videos, and the optional recovery update file.

TIP If a mode flashes or looks washed out, turn AUTO EV on before adjusting exposure manually.

RECOVERY

Camera not detected?

Disconnect other serial devices, reconnect the camera directly to the computer, and try again. If automatic reset fails, hold BOOT, tap RESET, release BOOT, then reconnect.

The release includes VALUECAM_SD.BIN for microSD recovery. Rename it to VALUECAM.BIN on the card.
